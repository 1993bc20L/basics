How To Achieve A Good Life?

1. Be Productive:

Your goal should be to produce something of worth and value for the world.

2. Value Relationships:

Collect and cultivate relationships with people who know you, know what you are about and like you.

Those who don't contribute to society often find that they pay a price in the form of solitude.

3. Respect Your Origins:

Know your culture, heritage, language and history

4. Spiritual Health:

No matter what you believe, it is important to spend time focussing on your spirituality and your beliefs.

5. Plant The Seeds:

God says; "If you plant the seed, i will make the tree." He just expects you to plant the seed, he will do the rest.


Do not let the hero in your soul perish in lonely frustration for the life you deserved and have never been able to reach. -Ayn Rand

Have an unyielding quest to be the best that you are capable of becoming, and always remember that bad decisions compound faster than good decisions.

I can make the better daily choices that will give me better daily results.
